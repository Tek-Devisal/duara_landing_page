Our Goals

We are driven by a clear mission: to create a cleaner planet by empowering people to take action against plastic pollution

Reduce Plastic Pollution Globally

Through Duara, we aim to significantly reduce plastic pollution by

  • incentivizing individuals to clean up their surroundings.
  • turning everyday walks into opportunities for environmental impact
  • removing tons of plastic waste from streets, parks, beaches, and other public spaces.

Promote Sustainable Living

Beyond cleanup efforts, Duara is committed to fostering a culture of sustainability.

Through our app, we provide users with educational resources, practical tips, and actionable insights to help them adopt eco-friendly habits. From reducing single-use plastics to recycling effectively, we aim to inspire long-term behavioral change that benefits the planet.

Track and Measure Progress

Transparency and accountability are core to our mission. Through real-time impact tracking, we provide,

  • users with detailed analytics on their contributions
  • personalized progress reports
  • insights into their environmental impact.

Drive Awareness and Education

We’re committed to raising awareness about the devastating effects of plastic waste on ecosystems, wildlife, and human health

Through in-app content, partnerships with environmental organizations, and community outreach, we aim to educate millions of people about the importance of reducing, reusing, and recycling.

Frequently Asked Questions

These are our frequently asked questions from our users

How do I earn cash rewards with Duara?

Simply capture images of plastic waste during your walks, upload them through the app, and earn cash rewards for each valid submission. The more you contribute, the more you earn!

What types of plastic waste can I submit?

You can submit images of plastic bottles, sachets, and other plastic waste. Ensure the waste is dry, presentable, and not from refuse dumps or gutters.

How does Duara verify the authenticity of my uploads?

Duara uses advanced AI technology to verify that images are authentic, unedited, and not duplicated. The app also checks for compliance with submission guidelines.

Can I participate in cleanup challenges with friends?

Yes! Duara encourages community engagement. You can join challenges with friends, share your progress, and collaborate on cleanup initiatives.

Is my personal information safe with Duara?

Absolutely. Duara prioritizes user safety and privacy, ensuring that your personal information is secure and only used for app functionality.

How do I withdraw my earnings?

You can withdraw your earnings directly through the app’s secure payment system. Follow the instructions in the "My Payments" section to transfer your funds.
